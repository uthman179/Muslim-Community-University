FUTURE PLANS



















Muslim University Community (MCU) is passionate about providing a learning environment that inspires its students. The 100-year-old grounds
and facilities have been carefully and respectfully enhanced to not only cater to the increasing University population but to ensure they promote
a desire for educational excellence. The University Masterplan 2023+ Designing for the Future is the renewed Masterplan future-proofing the
facilities and resources of the School. MCU will undertake the delivery of this Masterplan as a phased approach and the below highlights some of
the major works in progress, planned, or completed. The University has applied to the Department of State Development, Infrastructure, Local
Government and Planning (DSDILGP) seeking endorsement for a Ministerial Infrastructure Designation (MID) to improve the learning facilities for
our students over time.
