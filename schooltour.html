Self-guided Tour


You can still take a look at the MCU campus via our fully interactive self guided tour.


  • How to use the self-guided tour
  • After you have clicked ‘play’, click on the photo to view the menu interface
  • To view the tour in full screen, double click the photo
  • The small ‘i’ in a white circle indicates there is a video or more information about Stuartholme
  • Full virtual reality capability (VR glasses required) – to access VR mode, use the three dot menu located on the top right of the image
  • Compatible devices
  • Mobile an Tablet – touchscreen enabled
  • Laptop – mouse enabled
  • Load time is dependant on your internet connection
