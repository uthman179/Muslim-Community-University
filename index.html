Our Distinction


Muslim Community University is a Malaysian-based Islamic religious sect, founded in September,
1998 by 7 board members, volunteers from 7 Islamic centers in the surrounding area each serving
a different ethnic population (in an effort to represent the entire Muslim community). The University
is also known as MSU. We emphasize the use of the Arabic language, spoken and written, in order to
increase the awareness of the Qur'an and its meaning. Our religious program propagates the daily practice
of Islam as a comprehensive way of life, following the example of the Prophet Muhamad (peace be upon him).
Muslim Community University is part of the best University in the world .

Our Areas of Focus About islam








Qur’an & Hadith



Explanation of the sciences of the Qur’an
and Hadith for our times
firmly based on classical sources.




Aqidah





Islamic belief and theology with emphasis on Tawhid, Sifat (attributes) of Allah Most High and Islamic eschatology.




Fiqh





Easy to grasp courses on Islamic jurisprudence relating to purification, salat, zakat and fasting with special focus on contemporary issues.




Sirah





Enlightening courses on the life of the Messenger of Allah (Allah bless him and give him peace) and the four rightly-guided Caliphs.




Tazkiyah





Introduction of Islamic character and social etiquette focusing on developing praiseworthy character traits in an individual.
