Chancellor's Welcome Address



Read! In the name of your Lord who hath created; created man from a clot.
Read! And your Lord is the most bounteous, who teaches
by the pen, teaches man that which he knew not.(Qur’an 96:1-5)

Dear Parents,
Assalamu Alaikum.The very first verse that was revealed to our beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
by Almighty Allah outlines firstly the attribute of the One who creates and secondly the creation, who
is totally dependent on Allah. The verse also emphasizes that it is a duty on all Muslims to educate
themselves so that they may gain knowledge.The first word revealed was: READ! Reading is one
of the best ways, to gain knowledge. “Knowledge raises a human being to a higher position than
other creatures of creation.

Muslim Community University (MCU) is a leading institution dedicated to quality teaching, learning, research and community service. We have over the past few years
developed leaders in many disciplines through our various programmes. As the University of First Choice and the Nation’s Pride, we place great premium on the deve
-lopment and welfare of our staff and students both at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Since its establishment in 1962, the institution has continued to play
a key role in nation building by molding the teeming youth population through its teaching, research and community services. As the melting pot of the society, we stro
-ngly believe in diversity and, therefore, provide an atmosphere for an all-inclusive Campus. As a result, we attract quality staff and students who are able to compete
at the highest level locally, regionally and globally. This is evident in the over 200,000 highly skilled and knowledgeable graduates we have produced as professionals
contributing their quota in their respective endeavours.The University is a Private University uniquely positioned on the mainland of Lagos, the commercial nerve cen
-tre of Nigeria; with three Campuses in Akoka (Main Campus), Idi-Araba (College of Medicine and Faculty of Pharmacy) and Yaba (School of Radiography). The mai
-n campus is situated by the Lagos Lagoon, which makes it an attraction for tourists due to the serene view.Our programmes and courses are carefully developed to
convert students’ ingenuity and creative ideas into sustainable careers. Currently, we have twelve Faculties, namely, Arts, Basic Medical Sciences, Clinical Sciences,
Dental Sciences, Education, Engineering, Environmental Sciences, Law, Management Sciences, Pharmacy, Science, and Social Sciences. MCU, as the University is
fondly called, also offers Masters and Doctorate degrees in most of its programmes. We offer Open Distance Learning (ODL) programmes in Accounting, Business
Administration, Science Education and Library/Information Sciences through the Distance Learning Institute (DLI). Evening programmes are also available through
the Institute of Continuing Education (ICE) in the Faculties of Arts, Social Sciences, Business Management and Education. We have developed a robust research
culture where our academic staff and students focus on solving societal challenges through evidence-based and translational research. The University has various
Research Centres that address specific and topical issues germane to the society. Two of these Centres are the African Research Universities Alliance (ARUA)
Centre of Excellence which host 16 other African universities with common research vision. Our students have often taken advantage of our programmes and
location to activate their potentials. The Entrepreneurship and Skills Development Centre also provides students with the much needed avenue to develop their
innovative ideas for self-sufficiency and converting opportunities to business enterprises before completing their degrees.I am pleased to welcome you to the
Muslim Community University, the University of First Choice and the Nation’s Pride. Let me assure you that choosing MCU is a critical decision that would help you
flourish in life.


GIWA UTHMAN
Chancellor of Muslim Community University (MCU)
