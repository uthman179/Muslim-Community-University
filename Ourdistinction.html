ABOUT US








Muslim Community University is a Malaysian-based Islamic religious sect, founded in September, 1998 by 7 board members, volunteers from 7 Islamic centers
in the surrounding area each serving a different ethnic population (in an effort to represent the entire Muslim community). The University is also known
as MSU. We emphasize the use of the Arabic language, spoken and written, in order to increase the awareness of the Qur'an and its meaning. Our religious
program propagates the daily practice of Islam as a comprehensive way of life, following the example of the Prophet Muhamad (peace be upon him). Muslim
Community University is part of the best University in the world .



The Smart Academy provide the best education in a rich Islamic environment where Islamic thought, practice, culture and knowledge of Islam are the dominant
factors of the school curriculum. Our aim is to nurture and empower pupils to become responsible Muslim citizens who contribute not only to their own
development, but also to the well-being of their family, friends, neighbors and the community they live in.
